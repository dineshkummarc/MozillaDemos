T E T R I S   \|/   ORIENTATION

A part from the arrow keys on laptops you can orientate your device to control the pieces.
  • Lean the phone to the front to make the piece fall faster.
  • Lean the phone backwards to rotate the piece.
  • Lean the phone to the left to move the piece to the left.
  • Lean the phone to the right to move the piece to the right.
How far will you get on this game? Do your best as it's not easy :-)

  • Tap the Screen Rotation Lock button on the bottom left of the screen.
  • A padlock will appear in the Screen Rotation Lock button.
